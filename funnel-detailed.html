How to read the Lead row across categories

For the same city + week, the paid (Google call) and GMB (Organic) Lead counts will be IDENTICAL across MH, SH, and STD columns — the city paid phone + clinic GMB phones are a single shared inbound pool and the campaigns can't distinguish caller intent at the click. Web (UTM) leads ARE per-campaign UTM-attributed and differ. The category split kicks in below at Rel Lead (diagnosis-tagged subset), so the meaningful conversion comparison across categories starts at Rel Lead, then Book, then Done.

Known caveats

  • Jaipur STD w4 ROAS 54.7× is an artifact: campaign launched 2026-06-07 (last day of the window), spent only ₹68, one purchased patient gave ₹3,698. Treat as no-signal until at least a full week.
  • Bangalore MH w1 budget ₹1,380/d reflects the 2026-06-02 +15% budget scale (BLR went ₹1,200→₹1,380 on 06-02). The trailing weeks' Budget Util ratios assume the current daily rate, so w1's Util 50.9% is calculated against ₹1,380/d ≈ ₹9,660/wk.
