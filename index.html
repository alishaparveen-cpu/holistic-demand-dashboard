Path to 1000+ — actionable roadmap

Five levers grounded in your data. Adjust each lever's target to model scenarios. The running total updates live below.
Scenario composition
↗ Repeatable mechanics
  • FB sustained scale: replicate the 4-10 May budget burst, but kept on continuously. Lift was +25% leads at constant 19% CVR → linear in spend. Prereq: fix the FB-spend Redshift feed (broken since Feb 9) so you can measure efficiency before scaling.
  • FB creative diversification: the spike came from a single push. Run 4-6 creative variants in parallel to find ones that beat 19% CVR.
  • Google Search push: peak 145, current 122. Small lever (+20-30/wk) but proven & cheap.
  • No-show reduction: 240 no-shows/wk = 33% leakage. Even 25% recovery = +60 bookings → done.
⚠ Ceiling-breakers (need investment)
  • Instagram + YouTube: current ~13 + 0 bookings/wk. Adjacent FB audience, untested.
  • City expansion: top-delta during spike was Practo Online (+213 total bookings). Replicate the virtual-clinic capacity model.
  • Conversion improvement on FB: 19% lead→booking. Better landing page / faster response time could lift to 22-25% — that's +60-100 bookings/wk on FB alone at current spend.
  • New top-of-funnel: WhatsApp click-to-chat, content marketing, doctor-referral programs. Anything that isn't FB/GS dependency.
Lever defaults are calibrated from your 13-week data. Adjust the inputs to test sensitivity — e.g. "what if FB only gets to 350?" or "what if conversion stays flat?". The roadmap chart updates instantly.
