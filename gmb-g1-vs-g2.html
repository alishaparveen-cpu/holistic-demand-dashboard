The 3 Root Causes
⚙️
Root Cause 1 · Review collection process is missing in G2 (not age)
G1 collects 30.1 reviews/month per clinic; G2 collects 13.5/month — a 2.2× gap that persists even after normalizing for clinic age. Bellandur is 21 months old and collects only 12.7/month. Arekere is 17 months old and collects 13.5/month. Both should be mature enough to have a strong process. The gap is operational, not structural.
⚔️
Root Cause 2 · Competitive headwind: Pune (3 G2 clinics) faces a 1,800-review monster
All 3 Pune clinics (Baner, Katraj, Chinchwad) compete against Vanad Clinic — 1,800 reviews at 5.0★. That's the hardest competitive market of any city we operate in. G1 cities face 494–923 review competitors. G2 Pune clinics need 17–19× more reviews just to reach competitive parity. They also cannibalise each other in the same map pack — 3 Allo clinics in one city split the rank signals.
⏱️
Root Cause 3 · G2 is younger on average, but not by enough to explain the gap
G1 avg age is 20.5 months; G2 is 12.2 months — a difference, but not the explanation. Bharathi Nagar in G1 is only 6.5 months old and has already collected 323 total reviews (49.7/month) — more per month than any G2 clinic. Age is a partial factor. The dominant factor is still process discipline.

Universal Gaps (affect all clinics equally)
🧪
STI/HIV searches: Allo is absent in every city (61–90% of searches)
Searches like "HIV test near me" and "STD test Bangalore/Chennai/Pune" are dominated by diagnostic labs (Orange Health, SAMBU Labs, Metropolis, Apollo). Allo does not appear. This is a systematic category gap — not a G1 vs G2 issue. It represents a large untapped intent pool across all cities. Fixing this requires adding explicit testing services to profiles and building STI-specific reviews.
🧠
Psychiatrist searches: Allo is near-invisible in metro cities (99%+ absent in BLR + Pune)
Only Coimbatore shows meaningful psychiatrist pack presence (30% absent = 70% present, avg rank #1.6). All metro cities show 99%+ absence. Vazhikatti MHC (3,200 reviews) in CBE and Dr Sumit Kendre (58 reviews, 5★) in Pune dominate. MH category is the largest whitespace in the ranking landscape.

💡
Allo's rating advantage is a moat — but only if review mass doesn't collapse it
Allo outrates every sexologist competitor in every city by 0.08–0.34★. This is a genuine differentiation. However, competitors like Vanad Clinic (Pune, 5.0★) and Dr Fayyaz (Mumbai, 4.9★) have reached rating parity while maintaining massive review volume advantages. The rating moat alone won't protect rank forever — volume must catch up.
🎯
The fix is not about G1 — it's about 3 specific G2 problems
G1 doesn't need structural intervention. The gap is owned by: (1) Bellandur + Arekere in BLR having zero operational review process, (2) all 3 Pune clinics facing a monster competitor requiring urgent velocity, and (3) Bharathi Nagar's deceleration since Jun 22 needing investigation to protect the peak it already achieved.
