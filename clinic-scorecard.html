Kept = bookings that reached a show/no-show decision (not rescheduled or cancelled away). Bookings→Kept is reschedule/cancel leakage; Kept→Done is the true show-up rate.
Why no lead→booking C2B%? This clinic feed has no trustworthy top-of-funnel inquiry count. The only lead table (main_source_wise_leads) logs ~40–65% of booked patients by source — it under-counts bookings and has no record of inquiries that never booked, so any "C2B" off it comes out above 100% (nonsense). A real conversion rate needs the call-log (exotel_calls, 1.7M rows) and chatbot inquiry events joined in — that's Phase 2. Everything in this funnel is live appointment data.
